Made In Austria

We are a traditional light manufactory located in the heart of Europe, in Vienna where we produce the exclusive Design by ORION light collection based on historical models. ORION combines classical style elements from prized Austrian crafts heritage with state-of-the-art technology to create innovative lights for contemporary interiors. We are an internationally working, fourth-generation family business passionate about designing spaces through light - whether in private living areas, on business premises such as offices, law firms and practices, or in the hospitality sector for restaurants and hotels.
ORION specialises in manufacturing large-scale luminaires and handling extensive light installation projects.
